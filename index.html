About.


I am a PhD student at Georgia Tech, advised by Heinrich Matzinger. I am also a researcher at DuckAI, EleutherAI, and CarperAI.


My research interests center around enhancing the reasoning capabilities of language models, particularly in the realm of mathematical reasoning, and exploring the potential of multimodal models.


Prior to this, I completed my BS and MS in Mathematics from the University of Chicago.
